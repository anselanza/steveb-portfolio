More About Me

Examples of my work.

I've completed projects for clients such as BMW, Telkom, Standard Bank and Unilever (via Ogilvy), amongst others. While I have also created apps and websites, I get most excited about the more unusual projects that require combining media, interaction and software. I've also included some personal projects in the mix, since I particularly enjoy combining music with technology.

What I do.

Creative Directing.

I have plenty of experience conceptualising creative projects for a variety of demanding clients, writing up proposals and doing presentations for pitches. I have directed not only creative coders but also graphic designers, 3D animation teams and video production.

Technical Directing.

'Sure, we can do that.' I have a broad knowledge of various technologies and I know how to make them work together. Whether it's coding up something myself, directing talented developers/engineers or researching the right Open Source solutions available, I can always make it happen.

Creative Coding.

I have been programming since I was 9 years old, and I've always found it a thoroughly satisfying activity. My interest has always been to use software in the service of creativity. My favourite frameworks are Processing (Java) and openFrameworks (C++).

Web Development.

Web technologies are almost always a part of the things I create. I am a huge fan of AngularJS and NodeJS for creating modern web applications. And I love Open Source development tools such as Yeoman, Gulp and Bower.

Design.

Although I often direct more experienced designers for bigger projects, I am also very skilled at UX design. My favourite tool is currently Bohemian Coding Sketch and I am also very handy at using Apple Motion to rapidly mock up animations.

Product Development.

In the last two years I have founded a sub-brand within my current company, specifically for creating software products that are aimed at the Eventing industry, where I have a lot of experience. We have deployed a successful Event App for multiple clients and also a VR product.

Who I Am.

My Passion.

Coding, creativity and music.

My Approach.

I think I've got an unusual mix of analytical/problem-solving obsession as well as creativity. Big fan of Agile software development methodologies.

My Personality.

I am just as happy spending hours working on something alone as I am leading a team or presenting work to clients. And, yes, I do love wearing a hat.

My Goals.

I want to enjoy a lifetime making delightful creative work using technology.

Get In Touch With Me.
